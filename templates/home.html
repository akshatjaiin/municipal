Powerful Features

Everything you need for efficient municipal governance in one unified platform

Location-Based Reporting

Citizens can report issues with precise GPS coordinates and photo evidence for accurate documentation and faster response.

Smart Routing

Automatic assignment of complaints to relevant departments based on category - sanitation, roads, electrical, and more.

Real-Time Tracking

Track complaint status from submission to resolution with detailed timeline logs and status updates.

Officer Management

Assign officers, track performance metrics, and manage escalation workflows for efficient resolution.

Facility Ratings

Citizens can rate public facilities like toilets, parks, and government buildings for quality monitoring.

Attendance Tracking

Monitor worker attendance with check-in/check-out system and location verification.
